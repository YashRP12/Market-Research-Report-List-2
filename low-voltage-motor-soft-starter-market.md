<p><p>In the low voltage motor soft starter market, there are two types of soft starters available: 2-phase controlled soft starters and 3-phase controlled soft starters. </p><p>2-phase controlled soft starters are designed to control the starting current of motors with two phases, ensuring a smooth and gradual increase in voltage to prevent damage to the motor and connected equipment. These soft starters are typically used in applications where two-phase power is available.</p><p>On the other hand, 3-phase controlled soft starters are specifically designed for three-phase motors, providing a controlled start-up that reduces voltage and current spikes. These soft starters are widely used in various industries to protect motors and achieve energy-efficient motor operation.</p></p>
