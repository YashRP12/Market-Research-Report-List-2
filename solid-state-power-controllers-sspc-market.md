SSPCs are available in two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase. </p><p>Single Phase SSPCs are designed to regulate power in single-phase systems, which are commonly used in residential and small commercial settings. They provide efficient power management and protection features for appliances, lighting, and other single-phase devices.</p><p>Three Phase SSPCs, on the other hand, are suitable for industrial and large-scale commercial applications that require higher power distribution. They efficiently manage and protect three-phase systems, which are commonly used in factories, power plants, and other heavy-duty electrical setups.</p></p>
