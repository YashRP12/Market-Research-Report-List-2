<p><p>The residential floor scrubber market consists of three main types: walk-behind floor scrubbers, ride-on floor scrubbers, and stand-on floor scrubbers.</p><p>Walk-behind floor scrubbers are manually operated machines that require the user to walk behind them while controlling their movements. They are ideal for smaller residential areas and offer ease of maneuverability.</p><p>Ride-on floor scrubbers are larger machines that allow the user to sit on them while operating. These are suitable for bigger residential spaces as they cover a larger area in less time, providing efficiency and productivity.</p><p>Stand-on floor scrubbers are similar to ride-on scrubbers, but the user stands on the machine instead of sitting. These machines offer enhanced visibility and control, making them suitable for residential areas with limited space.</p><p>Overall, these different types of floor scrubbers provide options for residential cleaning needs based on the size and requirements of the space.</p></p>
