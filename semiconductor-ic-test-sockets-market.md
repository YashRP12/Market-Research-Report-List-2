<p><p>Semiconductor IC test sockets market types include BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFN (Quad Flat No-leads), WLCSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package), and others. BGA sockets are used for testing chips with tiny balls on the underside, providing a reliable connection. QFN sockets support chips with exposed pads around the periphery, ensuring easy testing. WLCSP sockets are designed for chips directly mounted on circuit boards, testing their functionality. The "others" category consists of sockets for different chip packaging types, catering to specific testing requirements. These socket types enable efficient and accurate testing of semiconductor integrated circuits.</p></p>
