<p><p>Studio headphones are available in three market types: closed back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed back headphones have a sealed design that isolates sound, preventing audio leakage and reducing external noise interference. Semi-open back headphones combine some elements of closed and open back designs, providing a balanced sound with a slight airiness. Fully-open back headphones have an open design that allows air and sound to pass freely, offering a more spacious and natural sound experience. Each market type caters to different preferences, usage scenarios, and sound requirements of studio professionals.</p></p>
