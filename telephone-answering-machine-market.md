<p><p>The telephone answering machine market is divided into two types: corded answering machines and cordless answering machines. Corded answering machines are devices that are physically connected to the telephone line and often have a built-in tape recorder for recording messages. On the other hand, cordless answering machines operate wirelessly and are usually paired with a base unit that connects to the telephone line. Both types serve the purpose of recording incoming calls and messages when the user is unable to answer the phone.</p></p>
