<p><p>Magneto Resistive RAM (MRAM) is a type of non-volatile memory technology that retains data even when power is turned off. Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM are two market types of MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes an electrical current to change the magnetization state of its material, allowing for fast read and write operations. On the other hand, STT-MRAM employs a spin transfer torque effect to alter the magnetic state, enabling higher storage capacity and improved energy efficiency. Both types of MRAM offer potential benefits for various applications in the market.</p></p>
