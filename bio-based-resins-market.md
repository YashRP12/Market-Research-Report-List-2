<p><p>Bio-based resins are materials derived from renewable sources such as plants and biomass. The increasing environmental concerns and stringent regulations regarding the use of fossil fuels are driving the demand for bio-based resins. Governments across the globe are promoting the use of renewable resources and sustainable materials, which further boosts the market growth.</p><p>The packaging industry is a major consumer of bio-based resins due to their sustainability and ability to reduce carbon emissions. The demand for eco-friendly packaging materials is rising, particularly in the food and beverage sector, which is expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>In addition, the automotive industry is adopting bio-based resins for manufacturing lightweight components, thereby reducing fuel consumption and carbon emissions. The construction sector is also witnessing increasing adoption of bio-based resins for applications such as coatings and adhesives.</p><p>However, the high cost of bio-based resins compared to petroleum-based resins is a key challenge for market growth.
