<p><p>The milk ingredients market can be classified into various types, including milk powder, casein and caseinates, milk protein concentrates and isolates, and other markets. </p><p>Milk powder is obtained by evaporating milk to remove its water content, resulting in a powdered form. It is widely used in the food and beverage industry for applications such as baking, confectionery, and dairy products.</p><p>Casein and caseinates are derived from milk proteins and find applications in the production of cheese, yogurt, and other dairy products. They improve texture, stability, and taste in food products.</p><p>Milk protein concentrates and isolates are high-protein ingredients obtained through filtration processes. They are used in sports nutrition products, infant formula, and dietary supplements due to their high nutritional value.</p><p>The other market segment includes various milk ingredients such as lactose, whey protein, and whey powder, which are used in a wide range of food products, including bakery, sauces, and sports drinks.</p></p>
