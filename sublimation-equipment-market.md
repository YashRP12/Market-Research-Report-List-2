<p><p>Sublimation equipment refers to devices used in the process of sublimation, which involves converting a solid directly into a gas without going through the liquid phase. There are three main types of sublimation equipment: manual, electric, and pneumatic. Manual equipment requires human intervention and hands-on operation. Electric equipment utilizes electricity for heating and operation, providing a more automated experience. Pneumatic equipment, on the other hand, employs compressed air to power the sublimation process, offering increased efficiency and control. These different types cater to varying preferences and requirements in the sublimation industry.</p></p>
