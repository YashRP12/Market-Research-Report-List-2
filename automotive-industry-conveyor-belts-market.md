<p><p>The automotive industry utilizes various types of conveyor belts, including TPU, TPEE, PVC, PE, and others. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) belts offer excellent wear resistance and flexibility. TPEE (Thermoplastic Polyester Elastomer) belts provide high elasticity, durability, and resistance to chemicals. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) belts are cost-effective and easily customizable. PE (Polyethylene) belts are lightweight, offer durability, and are suitable for heavy loads. The market also includes other types of belts that meet specific requirements, such as silicone belts for high-temperature applications or rubber belts for enhanced grip.</p></p>
