<p><p>Protein purification market is categorized into various types. Ultrafiltration involves the separation of proteins based on size through a semipermeable membrane. Precipitation utilizes chemicals to selectively separate proteins from a solution. Chromatography employs a stationary phase to separate proteins based on their different physical properties. Electrophoresis separates proteins based on their electrical charge and size. Western blotting detects specific proteins in a mixture by using antibodies. Additionally, there are other techniques like affinity chromatography, gel filtration chromatography, and ion exchange chromatography used in protein purification.</p></p>
