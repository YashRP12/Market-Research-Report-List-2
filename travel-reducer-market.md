<p><p>The Travel Reducer Market can be classified into two types: Flange Mounted and Shaft Mounted. Flange Mounted travel reducers are designed to be directly mounted onto a flange, typically on the end of a motor or gearbox. They are easy to install and provide a compact solution for power transmission. On the other hand, Shaft Mounted travel reducers are attached directly to the shafts of an application, eliminating the need for additional couplings or mounting devices. They offer flexibility in positioning and allow for easy maintenance and replacement.</p></p>
