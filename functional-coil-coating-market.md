<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Functional Coil Coating market consists of three main types: Primer, Back Paint, and Topcoat. Primer is a coating applied to the metal substrate to enhance its adhesion properties and provide corrosion resistance. Back Paint, also known as reverse coating, is applied to the backside of the coil to provide additional protection. Topcoat is the final layer applied to the external side of the coil, enabling desired aesthetics and providing long-lasting durability against weather conditions. Each type serves a specific purpose in enhancing the performance and appearance of the coated metal.</p></p>
