<p><p>The fuel gas booster compressor market consists of four types of compressors: reciprocating compressors, geared compressors, centrifugal compressors, and others. Reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress the gas, while geared compressors employ gears to increase the gas pressure. Centrifugal compressors utilize high-speed rotating impellers for compression. Lastly, the "others" category includes compressors that do not fit into the above types, such as screw compressors or scroll compressors. These different compressor types cater to varied industry requirements for fuel gas compression.</p></p>
